Guide For Pratical Driving Test

Inspection Of Vehicle

Starting Of Vehicle

Road Sense

Vehicle Control

Shape Test

During inspection the following things are expected to be checked

  • Tyres should be checked whether they are in proper condition or not
  • Spare tyre should also be checked accordingly
  • Head lights, back lights, indicators should be checked whether they are working properly or not
  • Engine oil level should be checked through oil gauge/dipstick.
  • The applicant should also check whether the vehicle has sufficient fuel or not
After inspection of vehicle, the applicant should sit in the vehicle and is expected to follow the cockpit drill procedure. In cockpit drill, the following steps are required to be followed by the applicants
  • Ensure that all the doors are closed. If any of the door is open or half closed, the applicant should close it properly.
  • The applicant should adjust seat as per his/her convenience, if required.
  • The applicant should adjust side mirrors and back view mirror of the vehicle according to his/her seat position
  • After seat, steering and mirrors adjustment, the applicant should wear seat belt
  • Before starting of vehicle, the applicant should make sure that the hand brake is applied properly.

Starting Of Vehicle

  • The applicant should make sure the gear is in neutral position.
  • Clutch should be pressed and checked properly prior to switching on the ignition switch.
  • The applicant should then switch on the ignition and start the vehicle engine.
  • The ignition attempt must be balanced and controlled. It should neither be unnecessarily long nor too short
Vehicle UN-Parking
  • After starting the vehicle, the applicant is required to reverse the vehicle from parking space and bring it to driving lane.
  • The applicant should use proper indicator while turning the vehicle in reverse. For instance, if the applicant is turning the vehicle to the left side in reverse, he/she should use left side indicator
  • The applicant is expected to make proper / appropriate use of side/back mirror

Road Sense

  • The applicant should follow traffic rules and drive as if he/she is in real life traffic environment.
  • The applicant should observe all traffic signs and rules.
  • The applicant should join road after observing the traffic flow and signals etc.
  • Wrong/dangerous joining of road would result in straight disqualification.
  • The applicant should drive in one lane. He/ She may change lane afterproperly giving indicatorbefore turning
  • The applicant should use indicators properly and well in time before taking any turn or changing lane
  • At a roundabout, the applicant should enter the roundabout after observing the traffic patiently. He who is on the right has the right of way in the roundabout.
  • The applicant should use proper lane in the round about. For instance, if the applicant intends to go straight, he/she should use the left lane. If he /she intends to go to the right side, right lane should be used.
  • He/she shall use the proper indicators while entering / leaving the roundabout
  • Violating the right of way shall be straight disqualification

Vehicle Control

  • The applicant should demonstrate good control over the vehicle.
  • The applicant should apply accelerator, brake and clutch in appropriate manner. No jerks or switching off the engine should occur at any time.
  • The applicant must show full control over the steering.
  • The applicant should handle the steering with both hands.
  • The applicant shall demonstrate a straight reverse as asked by the examiner in which the applicant must have full control over the vehicle. Reverse should not be in zig zag manner
  • Failure in straight reverse shall be straight away disqualification.

Shape Test

  • The applicant will be asked to drive the vehicle forward and backward in “L” shape. Time limit for “L” shape will be 03 minutes.
  • The applicant will then be asked to move to the parallel parking test. In parallel parking test, the applicant shall demonstrate 02 types of parking i.e. first will be parallel parking and second will be garage parking. Time limit for parallel will be 05 minutes.
  • After “L” shape and parallel parking, the applicant shall be asked to go through the “?” shape test. Time limit for “?” shape will be 03 minutes.
  • After “?” shape test, the applicant shall be asked to go through “S” shape test. Time limit for “S” shape will be 03 minutes.
  • Touching any cone, crossing line, exceeding time limit, adjusting vehicle more than once shall be straight disqualification.
  • “L” shape test and parallel parking test will be for all M.CAR/JEEP/LTV/LTV-PSV applicants.
  • “?” shape test will be only for LTV/LTV-PSV applicants.
  • “S” shape test will be only for LTV-PSV applicants.
